VR experience

Immersive experience in Unity 3D, tailored for Oculus Quest through OpenXR standard. Audio engine is provided by SuperCollider, communicating to Unity via OSC protocol. A Python RNN automates changes in audio parameters and fx.

Unity VR

RingO Modulator

Granular synthesiser, with reverb and ring modulator fx. Controllable by means of GUI + 4 hardware elements: a keyboard to set the grain pitch, a mouthpiece for sound intensity, a knob for randomness of grain position and a button to switch trigger type.
